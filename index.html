How it works

Step 1

Find a match

Our AI technology suggests medical professionals that fits your search requirements, and also recommend medical professionals that are a likely match, based on your profile and medical record. You can also find a match by searching out your preferred medical professional by using the search tool.

Step 2

Book an Appointment

Found your preferred medical professional? Proceed to book for a consultation by filling out a consultation request form and making payment. You can also subscribe to any of our packages for a better consultation booking experience.

Step 3

Consultation

U&DOC ensures you have an excellent consultation experience by sending you prompt updates and reminders. You can have audio or video consultation and a recording can be made and sent to you on request.

Step 4

Post-Consultation Services

U&DOC post-consultation services includes access to vetted pharmacy stores for purchase of prescribed drugs, hospial referral processing, sourcing and purchase of medical devices and many more at discounted prices
